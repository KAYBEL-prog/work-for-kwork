How does it work ?

Science behind sculptra

Sculptra PLLA powder is reconstituted with sterile water and lidocaine and mixed well to become homogenous structure before injection. After preparation, doctor identifies the goals to treat the face with canula injections to distribute the product proportionally in the areas that will require stronger lifting structure. The treatment is relatively painless and comfortable and takes 15-20 minutes with planning.

After the injection, doctor will  perform 5 min thorough massage and will recommend to do the same for next 5 days, 5 times a day. It will help to avoid potential product accumulations in one area and risk of creation of collagen nodules under the skin.
